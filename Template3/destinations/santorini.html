About Santorini

The Jewel of the Aegean

Santorini, the most iconic of the Greek islands, is famous for its dramatic volcanic landscape, stunning sunsets, and white-washed buildings with blue domes. This romantic paradise offers breathtaking views of the caldera, pristine beaches, and world-class cuisine.

Famous Sunsets

Experience the most spectacular sunsets from Oia's famous viewpoints.

Volcanic Landscape

Greek Cuisine

Savor authentic Greek dishes and world-renowned local wines.

Unique Beaches

Discover black, red, and white sand beaches with crystal-clear waters.

Travel Tips for Santorini

Make the most of your Santorini adventure

Best Time to Visit

May to October offers the best weather, with July and August being peak season.

Getting There

Santorini Airport serves the island. Ferries also available from Athens and other islands.

Accommodation

Stay in Oia for sunsets, Fira for nightlife, or Imerovigli for tranquility.

Local Cuisine

Try fresh seafood, Greek salads, fava, and the famous Assyrtiko wine.
