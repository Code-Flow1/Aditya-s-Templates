About Maldives

Paradise on Earth

The Maldives, a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean, is renowned for its pristine white-sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and luxurious overwater bungalows. This archipelago of 1,200 coral islands offers the ultimate escape for relaxation, romance, and adventure.

Pristine Beaches

White sand beaches with crystal-clear turquoise waters perfect for relaxation.

Water Activities

World-class diving, snorkeling, and water sports in vibrant coral reefs.

Luxury Resorts

Overwater bungalows and 5-star resorts offering ultimate privacy and comfort.

Marine Life

Rich biodiversity with colorful fish, manta rays, and whale sharks.

Travel Tips for Maldives

Make the most of your Maldives adventure

Best Time to Visit

November to April offers the best weather with less rainfall and calm seas.

Getting There

Male International Airport serves Maldives. Seaplane transfers to resorts included.

Accommodation

Choose from overwater bungalows, beach villas, or water villas for ultimate luxury.

Local Cuisine

Fresh seafood, tropical fruits, and international cuisine at resort restaurants.
